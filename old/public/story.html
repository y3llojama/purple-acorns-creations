Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Maya Rivera, Purple Acorns began as a tiny studio in a reclaimed barn. Every piece is designed, cast, and finished by hand, using ethically‑sourced gemstones and recycled metals.

Our name reflects the unexpected beauty we find in nature – the purple‑hued acorn is a rare, magical symbol of growth and creativity.
